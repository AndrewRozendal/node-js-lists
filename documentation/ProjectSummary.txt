ICS 211 Project Description

Short Summary
Andrew Rozendal has decided to get into the dump truck business - Rozendal Hauling Co.
Rozendal Hauling Co. is a small local dump truck rental company.  It rents dump
trucks with their drivers.  It has a dozen dump trucks available for rental. 
Visiters to the site can see trucks available for rental.  There are details on
the size of each truck, the costs, and the duration available.  Visiters can then
reserve a truck for a period of time.

Pages Required Based on the Summary
Home Page - A screen that lists trucks
Trucks Page - A screen that shows details about each truck
Reservations Page - A reservation screen
AboutUs Page - Contact information and history
